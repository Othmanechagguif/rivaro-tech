What We Track

Every metric has a purpose

🔥
Calories Burned
High burn day? Your dinner automatically increases in calories to support recovery and prevent muscle catabolism.
😴
Sleep Quality
Poor sleep? Tonight's meal shifts toward tryptophan-rich foods and magnesium to improve your sleep quality tomorrow.
❤️
Heart Rate & HRV
Low HRV signals stress or fatigue. AI reduces training-day calories slightly and increases anti-inflammatory foods.
👣
Daily Activity
Step count and active minutes inform your carbohydrate allocation — more movement means more fuel.
🩸
Blood Oxygen (SpO2)
Low SpO2 may indicate poor recovery. AI flags it and adjusts meal timing to optimize oxygen-carrying capacity.
🌡️
Skin Temperature
Temperature spikes indicate illness or overtraining. RIVARO automatically switches to recovery meal mode.
